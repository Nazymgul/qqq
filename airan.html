Airan

Airan is very popular amongst all Turkic people, and not only amongst the Nomads of Kazakhstan. The process of preparation is quite simple, but its benefits are incredibly great. It was even considered to be the main food during long horse riding. When all other products were used up, thick and tasty ayran saved brave wanderers from hunger and thirst. It is prepared from almost all types of milk which is put through a boiling process, and then a special fermentation starter is added to the already cooled but still warm milk. After a strong shake, the drink is sent “to rest” for 2-3 hours during which it steeps, gets a density and a specific taste.
